# AEO Vision: The Ultimate Answer Engine Optimization Tool

AEO Vision is a world-class platform designed to help brands, startups, and marketers navigate the shift from traditional Search Engine Optimization (SEO) to **Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)**.

**Project Repository:** [https://github.com/AbrahamOjes/AEO.git](https://github.com/AbrahamOjes/AEO.git)

### 1. Omni-Model Visibility Tracking
*   **Feature:** Simultaneous querying of multiple AI models (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity).
*   **Performance Impact:** Eliminates "model bias." Brands can see where they are strong in conversational AI (ChatGPT) vs. search-grounded AI (Perplexity).

### 2. Deep Reasoning & Thinking Mode
*   **Feature:** Utilizes **Gemini 3 Pro** with a 32k token `thinkingBudget` for complex strategic analysis.
*   **Performance Impact:** Provides non-obvious, high-level insights that standard LLM turns miss.

### 3. AI-Powered Optimization Playbook
*   **Feature:** Generates a prescriptive strategic roadmap including specific actions, impact levels, and effort requirements.
*   **Performance Impact:** Moves the tool from *diagnostic* to *prescriptive*.

### 4. Competitive Intelligence & Market Grounding
*   **Feature:** Automated research of identified competitors using Google Search grounding.
*   **Performance Impact:** Identifies "content gaps" in the brand's digital footprint compared to market leaders.
